Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions about IPTV USA 4K? Find all our setup guides, account features, and technical answers here.

What is included in the IPTV USA 4K subscription?
Our premium IPTV USA subscription includes instant access to over 60,000 live HD and 4K television channels, more than 66,000 video-on-demand movies and complete series. It features a complete electronic program guide (EPG), premium sports networks, international programming, freeze-free technology, and 24/7 technical customer support.
How fast will I receive my IPTV connection credentials?
Your login details (M3U playlist, EPG link, and Xtream Codes API connection information) will be sent instantly to your email or WhatsApp within 10 to 15 minutes of completing your order.
Can I use the IPTV subscription on multiple devices simultaneously?
Yes, depending on the subscription package you purchase. The Basic Package supports 1 connection, the Premium Package supports 2 connections, and the Plus Package supports up to 4 devices streaming simultaneously. Please note that attempting to use more connections than permitted by your plan may lead to temporary suspension of your line.
Do I need a high-speed internet connection for 4K streaming?
For stable, buffer-free streaming, we recommend an internet connection speed of at least 25 Mbps for HD channels and 50 Mbps or higher for our dedicated 4K and Ultra HD streams. A wired Ethernet connection to your streaming box is always preferred over Wi-Fi for maximum stability.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), secure PayPal transactions, and popular cryptocurrency options for added privacy. Contact our support via WhatsApp for special billing configurations.
What is your refund policy?
We offer a 100% money-back guarantee. If you experience technical problems or channel delivery issues that our support team cannot resolve within 7 days of purchase, we will refund your order in full.
Do you support local channels and pay-per-view (PPV) sports?
Yes, our subscription includes local network channels (NBC, CBS, ABC, FOX) for all major USA cities, as well as premium sports networks (ESPN, Sky Sports, DAZN, Bein Sports) and live coverage of major PPV boxing, UFC, and WWE events at no extra cost.
