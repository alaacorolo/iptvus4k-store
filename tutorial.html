IPTV Setup & Installation Guides

Configure your IPTV USA subscription in less than 5 minutes. Select your device below for tailored step-by-step instructions.

Amazon Firestick IPTV Setup Guide

Setting up your IPTV USA subscription on an Amazon Fire TV Stick is best done using the **TiviMate Player** or **IPTV Smarters Pro** app. Follow these easy steps:

1

Install the Downloader App

From your Firestick Home Screen, go to the Find menu, click Search, type "Downloader", and download/install the app (orange icon).

2

Enable Apps from Unknown Sources

Go to your Firestick Settings > My Fire TV > Developer Options. Turn on Install Unknown Apps for the Downloader app. *(If Developer Options is hidden, go to About and click your Fire TV Stick name 7 times rapidly to unlock it)*.

3

Download IPTV Smarters Pro

Open Downloader, enter the direct download code `78522` (or type `smarters` link) to download the IPTV Smarters APK. Click Install once the download completes.

4

Enter Your IPTV USA 4K Credentials

Open the app, select Login with Xtream Codes API, and input the connection details we sent to your email/WhatsApp (Username, Password, and Server Portal URL). Click **Add User** and enjoy your channels!

Need a different app? TiviMate is highly recommended for an premium cable-like TV Guide interface. You can download it through Downloader using code `272483` and enter your M3U playlist link to start streaming.

Smart TV IPTV Setup Guide (Samsung & LG)

For Samsung and LG Smart TVs, we recommend using the **IBO Player** or **Smart IPTV** app directly from the TV's official App Store.

1

Search for IBO Player

Open your TV's App Store (Samsung Smart Hub or LG Content Store), search for IBO Player, and install it.

2

Retrieve Your TV MAC Address

Launch the IBO Player app on your TV. You will see a screen showing your Device MAC Address and Device Key. Note these down.

3

Upload Your Playlist Link

Go to the IBO Player portal (`https://iboplayer.com/device/login`) on your phone or PC. Enter your MAC Address and Device Key, then paste the M3U Playlist Link provided in your subscription email.

4

Restart Your TV App

Go back to your Smart TV, reload the IBO Player application, and all live TV categories and movies will be ready to stream.

Android TV & Mobile IPTV Setup Guide

Android boxes, Nvidia Shield, and Android smartphones have native access to premium IPTV players directly through the Google Play Store.

1

Download IPTV Smarters Pro

Open the Google Play Store on your Android box or phone, search for IPTV Smarters Pro (or **XCIPTV**), and download it.

2

Open and Choose Connection Option

Launch the app and click on Login with Xtream Codes API (the most stable connection method).

3

Fill in Your Xtream Codes Data

Enter the Username, Password, and Server Portal URL sent to you when you bought your plan. Click **Add User**.

Apple TV, iPad & iPhone IPTV Setup Guide

For iOS and Apple TV platforms, we recommend **IPTV Smarters Pro** or the premium **Smarters Player Lite** application available on the App Store.

1

Download Smarters Player Lite

Open the Apple App Store, search for Smarters Player Lite (or **IPTV Smarters Pro**), and download it to your device.

2

Login with Xtream Codes API

Open the app, accept the terms, select the option to load playlist via Xtream Codes API, and enter your login details.

3

Load and Watch

Click Login. Wait a few seconds for the app to sync with our servers and load the live channels database. Happy viewing!
